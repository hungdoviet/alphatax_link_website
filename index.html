Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our taxation, accounting, and business advisory services.

What documents do I need to prepare for my tax return? ➕

You'll need your income statements (PAYG summaries), records of any work-related expenses, bank statements, private health insurance details, and documentation for any investment income or capital gains.

How can Alpha Taxlink help my small business with ATO compliance? ➕

We manage all aspects of small business tax including BAS preparation, GST compliance, PAYG payroll setup, superannuation obligations, and annual financial statements to keep you fully ATO-compliant.

Do you provide bilingual accounting services? ➕

Yes, our expert team provides professional, bilingual accounting and tax services in both English and Vietnamese, which is ideal for migrant investors and diverse business owners.

What is the process for setting up a new business structure in Australia? ➕

We guide you through selecting the right entity (Sole Trader, Partnership, Company, or Trust), register your ABN, TFN, and GST, and set up your accounting system to ensure a smooth, compliant start.

How often do I need to lodge my Business Activity Statement (BAS)? ➕

Most businesses lodge BAS quarterly, with due dates in October, February, April, and July. We help prepare and lodge your BAS on time to avoid penalties.
