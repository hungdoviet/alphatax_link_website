3. Investments & Property

Rental property annual income/expense statements, quantity surveyor depreciation schedules, CGT purchase/sale contracts.

Questions & Answers

Tax Return FAQs

Common questions answered by our Footscray tax accounting team.

When is the deadline to lodge an individual tax return in Australia? ➕

For individuals lodging on their own, the deadline is 31 October. However, if you lodge through Alpha Taxlink as your Registered Tax Agent, you may qualify for an extended deadline up to 15 May of the following year.

What deductions can I claim on my individual tax return? ➕

You can claim work-related expenses directly related to earning your income, such as vehicle costs, working-from-home expenses, tools, uniforms, self-education, and professional memberships, provided you have receipts or appropriate documentation.

How does Alpha Taxlink handle company and sole trader tax returns? ➕

We reconcile your profit & loss statement, optimize allowable business expense write-offs, apply asset depreciation rules, calculate motor vehicle deductions, and ensure full ATO compliance for companies, sole traders, and trusts.

Can you help me if I have overdue or late tax returns from previous years? ➕

Yes, our team specializes in lodging multi-year overdue tax returns, liaising directly with the ATO to request penalty remissions and establish realistic payment plans.
