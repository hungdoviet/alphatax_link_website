Questions & Answers

Business Advisory FAQs

Answers to common business setup and strategy inquiries.

Which business structure is best for my business in Australia? ➕

The ideal structure depends on your liability exposure, growth plans, and tax optimization goals. A Sole Trader structure is simple to start, whereas a Proprietary Limited (Pty Ltd) Company or Trust structure provides legal asset protection and capped corporate tax rates.

How does proactive tax planning benefit small businesses? ➕

Proactive tax planning takes place before the end of the financial year (June 30). We analyze your projected income, evaluate allowable write-offs, optimize superannuation contributions, and structure profit distributions to minimize your overall tax burden legally.

What is included in Alpha Taxlink's new business setup package? ➕

Our business setup package includes ASIC company registration, ABN, TFN, GST, and PAYG registration, trust deed establishment, company register binder, and cloud accounting software integration.

Do you offer bilingual business advisory for Vietnamese business owners? ➕

Yes, our CPA qualified advisors offer fluent bilingual consultation in English and Vietnamese, assisting migrant entrepreneurs and business owners navigate Australian commercial regulations with confidence.
